Pitch

Play as the only canine transported in a foreign Japanese-like feline world. Collect and expand your deck of magical cards and refine your sleight of hand to find your way back home.

Description

Once upon a time, there was a young and talented dog magician named Ace, performing his best magic tricks on stage. Suddenly, POOF! His assistant opens a portal by mistake. Ace slips and lands in the private chamber of the Cat Empurror himself, and sees something he shouldn't have. The embarrassed Empurror brands Ace a criminal. Ace escapes the castle only to find themselves in a cat-filled Japan-like kingdom. The secret to getting home is in the Empurror's chambers.
